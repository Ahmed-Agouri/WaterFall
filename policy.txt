User policy and rules for Waterfall

Welcome to Waterfall, a social media platform designed to connect people and share information. To ensure that our community remains safe and enjoyable for everyone, we have established this User Policy to outline the expectations for behavior on our platform.

By using Waterfall, you agree to abide by the following rules:

No Inappropriate Content: You agree not to post, upload, or share any content that is illegal, defamatory, harmful, hateful, or otherwise inappropriate. This includes, but is not limited to, images or videos containing graphic violence, nudity, or sexually explicit material, as well as comments that promote hate or discrimination against any person or group.
Respect Others: You agree to treat others with respect and refrain from posting any content that is harassing, bullying, or otherwise intended to harm or offend others.
Protect Privacy: You agree to respect the privacy of others and not share any personal information without their consent. This includes, but is not limited to, addresses, telephone numbers, and email addresses.
Comply with Laws: You agree to comply with all applicable laws and regulations, including but not limited to, those related to intellectual property, data privacy, and online speech.
No Spam or Misleading Content: You agree not to post any content that is spammy, misleading, or otherwise intended to deceive others.
Violation of these rules may result in the removal of the offending content and/or the suspension or termination of your account. Waterfall reserves the right to take any action it deems necessary to protect its community and maintain a safe and enjoyable experience for all users.
